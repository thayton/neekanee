About Neekanee

Neekanee is a job search engine that collects jobs directly from company web sites. Unlike traditional job boards, companies do not have to pay to have their jobs listed on Neekanee and job seekers do not have to wade through the spam that plagues traditional job boards. All of the links in the search results take you directly to the original job description on a company's website.
